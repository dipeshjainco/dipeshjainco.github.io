Regulatory Approvals & Representations

Secretarial Audit

Compliance Management ensures organizational efficiency, transparency as well as investor confidence. Ever evolving laws and regulations have placed immense responsibilities on Directors and Managerial Personnel of Companies who must protect the interest of all stakeholders.

Thus, audit of records and documents by an external professional services firm is vital in ensuring that all required compliances are in place and in seeking advice on the corrective measures that need to be taken, in case discrepancies or incidents of non-compliance are noted.
